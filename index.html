Present Simple Verb Rules

Regular Verb

Drink
I drink We drink
You drink You (all) drink
He, she, it drinks They drink

Verbs with "s" Sound (s,ch,sh,x,z)

Watch
I watch We watch
You watch You (all) watch
He, she, it watches They watch

Consonant + "y"

Try
I try We try
You try You (all) try
He, she, it tries They try

Pronounce these:

Do: du Does: dʌz
Go: goʊ Goes: goʊz

"Be" is always special

Be
I am We are
You are You (all) are
He, she, it is They are
